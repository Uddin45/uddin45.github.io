About

I am a PhD researcher in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Windsor. My work focuses on deep reinforcement learning and multi-agent decision systems for large-scale cyber-physical networks. I study how learning-based algorithms can enable efficient coordination and resource allocation in dynamic environments such as vehicular edge computing systems.

Research Interests

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning
  • Vehicular Edge Computing
  • Wireless AI
  • Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Resource Allocation and Optimization

Research Projects

  • Adaptive Reward Clustering and Sampling (ARCS)

  • Priority-Aware Task Offloading in Vehicular Edge Computing
    Multi-agent reinforcement learning framework for resource allocation and task scheduling in dynamic wireless networks.

    Hierarchical learning framework integrating reinforcement learning with game-theoretic optimization.
