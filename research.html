Research

Deep Reinforcement Learning for Vehicular Edge Computing

Developing reinforcement learning frameworks for efficient task offloading and resource allocation in vehicular edge computing environments. The goal is to improve system efficiency while reducing latency and energy consumption in dynamic wireless networks.

Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Designing cooperative and decentralized multi-agent reinforcement learning methods for distributed decision making in wireless cyber-physical systems. This research investigates coordination strategies among agents under dynamic network conditions.

Adaptive Reward Clustering and Sampling (ARCS)

A reinforcement learning experience sampling mechanism that improves training stability and convergence efficiency by clustering experiences based on reward characteristics.

Wireless AI and Cyber-Physical Systems

Applying artificial intelligence techniques to optimize communication and computation resources in next-generation wireless networks and cyber-physical systems.
